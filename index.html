Work in Progress

Current Approach: This tool uses a rule-based method that counts positive and negative words from predefined dictionaries. While useful for basic sentiment detection, this approach has limitations.
Accuracy Limitations: The current version may not accurately interpret context, sarcasm, complex emotions, or nuanced language. For example, "not good" might be counted as positive due to the word "good."
Future Improvements: We're working on enhancing accuracy through better natural language processing, context awareness, and handling of negations and complex sentence structures.
Use as a Guide: Consider this tool as a starting point for understanding your text's tone, but always use your own judgment for important communications.
